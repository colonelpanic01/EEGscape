# EEGscape
Connect your Muse cranium headset to play games with your mind! 
Tilt, blink, and focus your way through 3 unique games. You won't have to lift a finger!

## Technology
EEGscape is built using:
- React
- Vite
- Tailwind
- DaisyUI
- MuseJS
